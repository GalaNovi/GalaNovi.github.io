Purchase process help

  • Placing an Order

    Once you've selected a product, select from the available purchase options, such as: Questions & Answers - Testing Engine product, PDF Questions & Answers or physical shipment of Hard Copy. Follow the prompts to complete your order. Note: Your steps may vary from these listed here, depending on the page(s) where you started.

    1. Select the product(s) you would like to order and select from the available options such as: Testing Engine, PDF Questions & Answers, Royal Packs and click the Add to Cart button.
    2. Your shopping cart will display a list of the product(s) you have ordered. To add more items to your cart Continue Shopping. Once you are finished selecting Products click the Cart button on Top Navigation Bar.
    3. Fill in the Billing Information using the correct Information and click on Pay Now Button to proceed to payment. Before clicking the Pay Now button make sure that all information is Correct. A receipt page displays once you have successfully placed an order. You will then be asked to create login with Testking.com. If you currently have a Testking account, you may log in through the Log In Now tab and then proceed to Checkout.
    4. Enter your Account Information for the fields (* indicates required fields), and then click the Continue button.
    5. Click the Install from My Account link or the Go to My Account Now button to install your products.
  • Reviewing Your Selections

    Your shopping cart lists the product(s) you have selected, along with quantities and totals. No Taxes are applied to Testking Products. You may remove any products from your shopping cart by clicking the "Trash" button in the row of the item you would like to remove. Click the Continue Shopping button to add more items to your shopping cart or the Check Out button once you are done shopping and would like to proceed to checkout.

    Note: After clicking the Continue Shopping button, you can add any other retail product to your cart, you will be redirected to the Testking All Products Online Store.

  • Payment Options

    We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express and, in some countries, JCB. We also accept PayPal, Moneybookers, Bank Wire Transfer and Western Union.

  • Billing Information

    When entering your credit card number on the Billing Information page, do not include spaces or dashes. Once you have completed the required information, click the Pay Now Button to proceed. Please do not click the "Pay Now" button more than once.

  • Creating an Account

    After checking out, you will be asked to create an account with Testking, if you don't already have one. An account is required for accessing the Products.

    Complete the customer profile form and review the privacy policy. Click the Submit Button and your account will be created.

    Remember, the email address used to create your account on Testking is your account ID. Use this email address to log in whenever you return to the Testking Web site.

    If you currently have a Testking Account, you may log in through the Log In Now link.

  • Renewing Expired Product

    Whenever you purchase a Product, our system begin the 90 day validation period. After Expiry the Products will still show in your login and you can renew them at 40% discount just by click on the "Renew Now" button next to each Expired Product. Your shopping cart will show Product Renewal at 40% Discount. You may now proceed to check out.

  • Downloading and Installing

    After Purchase is complete, you will need to download and install your products from Testking My Account by clicking on the relevant Download/Install/Update button(s).
